Empowering Communities. Building Opportunities.

Welcome to Ascension Building Services (ABS), a mission-driven consultancy dedicated to uplifting underserved communities through inclusive business development, strategic planning, and workforce training. Founded in Massachusetts and led by a neurodivergent CEO, ABS supports minority-owned, non-English-speaking, and neurodivergent-led businesses with customized solutions that drive sustainable growth.

What We Offer

Inclusive Business Development

Support for minority-owned, neurodivergent, and multilingual entrepreneurs to structure and grow sustainable businesses.

Operational Efficiency & Growth
