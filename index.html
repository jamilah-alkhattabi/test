Executive Summary

Ascension Building Services LLC (ASB) is a full-service business consulting company headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts. Founded in 2013, ASB has become a trusted advisory source for clients across various industries by providing exceptional Procurement, Development, Project Management, and Business Support solutions.

At Ascension, our clients' success is our ONLY success. We work alongside clients as partners to help them achieve unprecedented levels of growth and success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to challenge the status quo – to help leaders and teams rethink and reshape their organizations’ approach to people, processes, and technology while challenging our consultants to grow as leaders in the business community.

Enterprise Growth and Transformation

Whether there is a problem to be solved or a new business strategy to reflect your envisioned future, our consultants and industry expert partners will guide your organization with the key assessments and tools that enable actionable outcomes and strategies.

Strategic Planning and Execution

Evolving customer expectations and emerging technologies pose challenges like never before. We create and execute workforce management strategies that optimize productivity and engagement across your organization.
