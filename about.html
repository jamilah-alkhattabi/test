Who We Are

Aja Tsige leads an accomplished team with over 20 years of experience in business development and government procurement. Our company is dedicated to helping businesses navigate the complexities of the public and private sectors. We specialize in providing tailored solutions that drive growth, enhance operational efficiency, and secure critical contracts.

Our services range from securing government contracts to guiding businesses through the certification process as minority or small business enterprises. Whether you're a small business owner looking to break into government contracting or an established company seeking to optimize operations, we are here to guide you every step of the way.
