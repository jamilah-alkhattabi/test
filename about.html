About Ascension Building Services

Founded by Aja Tsige, Ascension Building Services is a mission-driven business consultancy based in Massachusetts. We specialize in empowering minority-owned, neurodivergent-led, and non-English-speaking entrepreneurs through inclusive business development, operational strategy, and workforce training. Our model bridges strategic consulting with community-based empowerment, helping organizations thrive sustainably.

Through partnerships with community organizations, housing developers, and workforce alliances, we support economic mobility and equity for those often left out of traditional systems. Our work includes digital resource development, grant support, green trade training, and inclusive entrepreneurship programs.
