Who We Are

Ascension Building Services is a full-service consulting firm specializing in strategic planning, construction management, community empowerment, and workforce development. We offer hands-on support to nonprofits, entrepreneurs, and small businesses facing barriers to access and growth.

Our Mission

To empower and support underserved, minority-owned, and neurodivergent-led businesses with resources and innovative strategies that create lasting community impact.

Our Vision

To scale a digital-first consultancy that delivers sustainable development, inclusive workforce training, and business tools to uplift diverse communities across the nation.
